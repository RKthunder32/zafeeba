Our Story

Crafting Luxury Since 2010

Zafeeba was born from a passion for creating fragrances that tell a story. Our journey began in a small perfumery in Paris, where our founder experimented with rare ingredients to create unique scent profiles.

Today, we continue this tradition of excellence, sourcing the finest ingredients from around the world to craft perfumes that embody elegance and sophistication.

Sarah Zafeeba

Founder & Master Perfumer

With over 15 years of experience in the fragrance industry, Sarah has dedicated her life to the art of perfumery. Her journey began at the prestigious Grasse Institute of Perfumery in France, where she mastered the delicate balance of scent composition.

"Perfume is more than just a scent - it's an emotion, a memory, a story waiting to be told. At Zafeeba, we craft not just fragrances, but wearable art that becomes part of your identity."
