Meet Our Founder

Afaq Khan

Founder & Perfume Artisan

With over 15 years of experience in the fragrance industry, Afaq Khan founded Zafeeba with a vision to create perfumes that tell a story and evoke emotions. His passion for exceptional scents and commitment to quality has made Zafeeba a respected name in luxury perfumes.

Afaq's journey began in the fields of Grasse, France, where he learned the art of perfumery from master artisans. Today, he continues to personally oversee every fragrance creation, ensuring each bottle meets the highest standards of excellence.
